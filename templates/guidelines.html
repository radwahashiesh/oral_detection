Clinical Guidelines

Image Guidelines

  • Use high-quality, well-focused images
  • Ensure proper lighting without shadows
  • Include the entire lesion in frame
  • Use consistent magnification when possible
  • Avoid image compression artifacts

Description Guidelines

  • Include key histological features
  • Describe clinical presentation
  • Note patient symptoms when relevant
  • Include location and size information
  • Mention any relevant medical history
Best Practices
  • Always correlate AI results with clinical examination
  • Use histological confirmation for definitive diagnosis
  • Consider multiple differential diagnoses
  • Document all findings in patient records
  • Consult with pathology specialists when needed
